Features

A whole club, in one app.

From January sign-ups to the trophy ceremony: Control runs the championship so you can run the races.

01

Standings

Overall and by cup, with medals per event, drop scores and gross or net points. Always up to date after every race day.

02

Drivers & teams

Profiles with search and categories (Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze). Your whole club roster in one place.

03

Events & calendar

The season's race days with their results, connected to what Manager times.

04

Scrutineering

A log of the cars' technical checks, so a level playing field isn't a matter of faith.

05

Treasury

Fees, registrations and championship movements with a running balance. Clean books, no separate spreadsheets.

06

Credits

A credit balance per driver, to manage payments and spending inside the club.

07

Publish

Standings to your WordPress site in one click, plus PDF export to print or share.

08

In the cloud, if you want

Google Sheets sync, light or dark theme, and just as comfortable on mobile as on desktop.
