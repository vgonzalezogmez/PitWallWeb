The engine of
the race.

PitWall Manager is the brain of the system: it talks to the timer —DS-300 over serial port or BART over Bluetooth LE—, counts every crossing, calculates times and statistics, and acts as a web server: Lap, Control and any device the driver or team owns live the race in real time.

Features

Full control, in one room.

Manager runs on your computer, right by the track. No internet, no cloud: your hardware, your WiFi network and the race.

01

DS-300 over serial

Direct connection to the DS-300 timer over COM port, with a purpose-built parser and an instant alert if the signal drops mid-race.

02

BART over Bluetooth

Compatible with the BART system (Slot.it / Policar) over Bluetooth LE: master + slaves to grow up to 32 lanes.

03

Three race views

Horizontal rows, compact grid or detailed cards. Pick the view that fits your lanes and your screen size.

04

Projected standings

Laps projected to the end of the race, gap to the car ahead and the pace needed to catch it. In its own window, ready to project.

05

Fastest laps

A live strip with each lane's best lap, always on screen while the heat is running.

06

Endurance

Breaks, relays and lane rotation for long 24-hour-style races, with the rotation sequence always in sight.

07

Lap correction

Add or remove crossings without stopping the race. Final results export ready to share or score.

08

Pole & structure

The whole race as one flow: pole position, rounds and heats, then results. Every phase knows when it's up.

On screen

Made to be read from afar.

Big numbers, gold for what matters right now and green for whoever is on track. The overall standings live in their own window, ready for the projector.
