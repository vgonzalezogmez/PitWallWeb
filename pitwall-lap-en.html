Features

No wires. No setup.

Open the app, it finds the race, and the times start coming in. Everything else is details built for real racers.

01

Automatic connection

Discovers the Manager server on your network in seconds. Not showing up? Enter the IP once and it remembers it.

02

Voice callouts

The app talks to you while you race: laps, position, gaps, 2-minute average, last minute… You choose what it calls out.

03

Rest mode

Not racing this heat? It tells you straight: your next heat, your lane, and the voice stays silent until it's your turn.

04

Live pole

During pole you see your time in big digits and the provisional standings with your row highlighted, lap after lap.

05

Practice

Record stints with your car's data (model, crown/pinion) and compare 2–4 stints with overlaid charts.

06

Offline history

Every race dossier stays saved on your phone: final standings, laps and the comparison Excel ready to download.

07

For the whole team

Built for drivers, pit crews and track marshals. The server is web-based: follow the race from any of your devices, all at once.

08

iOS & Android

The same app on both platforms, connected over WiFi to the server in the room. No internet, no accounts.
